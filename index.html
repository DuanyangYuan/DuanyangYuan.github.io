About Me

Hi there, I am Duanyang Yuan. I am currently a Ph.D. student at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), where I conduct research on intelligent decision-making methods for complex environments. I received my M.S. degree from Shanghai University (2021–2024).

My research interests lie at the intersection of Planning and Decision Making, Large Language Models, and Knowledge Graphs. During my Ph.D. studies, I am fortunate to be advised by Researcher Jian Huang and Associate Researcher Sihang Zhou.
