About Me

Hi there, I am Duanyang Yuan (袁端阳). I am currently a Ph.D. student at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), where I conduct research on intelligent decision-making methods for complex environments. I received my M.S. degree from Shanghai University (2021–2024). My research interests are the intersection of planning and decision making, large language models, knowledge graphs, and information retrieval. In particular, I study how structured knowledge and retrieval can be integrated to improve the reliability and adaptability of reasoning in LLM-based systems. During my Ph.D. studies at NUDT, I am fortunate to be advised by Researcher Jian Huang and Associate Researcher Sihang Zhou.
